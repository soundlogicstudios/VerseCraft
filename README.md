# VerseCraft

**Narrative RPG Platform — System-First, Story-Driven**

VerseCraft is a **mobile-first narrative RPG platform** that blends classic choose-your-own-adventure storytelling with real RPG mechanics: stats, inventory, failure, replay, and consequence.

This is **not** a tech demo, **not** an AI story toy, and **not** a one-off indie game.  
VerseCraft is a **coherent system** designed to scale stories without scaling chaos.

---

## What Is VerseCraft?

VerseCraft lets players experience stories where:
- Choices change *who you are*, not just what happens
- Inventory and preparation matter
- Failure is meaningful and instructive
- Replay reveals genuinely new paths

It’s built around a **shared ruleset** that works across genres, allowing many worlds to exist without reinventing mechanics every time.

## Current Status (What’s Already Done)

This project is **past the idea stage**.

### ✅ Product Vision Locked
- Clear positioning: **Narrative RPG platform**
- Not “interactive fiction”
- Not AI-generated content
- Not branching text for novelty

The design problem is solved and stable.

---

### ✅ Core Ruleset Frozen (v1.0)
No moving goalposts.

**Inventory limits**
- 20 consumables  
- 9 weapons  
- 9 armors  
- 9 special items  

**Design principles**
- Failure is part of progression
- Replay is intentional, not cosmetic
- Choices are gated by player state
- Systems teach themselves through play

This dramatically reduces engineering churn.

---

### ✅ Flagship Demo Defined
**Lorecraft: The World That Notices**

- 60–80 sections
- Free, canonical onboarding experience
- Narrative-driven tutorial (no UI-heavy explanations)
- Multiple endings + replay recognition

Lorecraft serves as:
- Player onboarding  
- Proof of concept  
- Platform thesis  

---

### ✅ Story Scalability Proven
- Multiple long-form stories already written (150+ sections)
- Multiple genres tested against the same system
- Proven that the ruleset holds without breaking tone

Scalability is demonstrated, not theoretical.

---

### ✅ Scope Is Intentionally Tight
Early VerseCraft will **not** include:
- Multiplayer or co-op
- Procedural generation
- Complex skill trees
- Creator marketplace
- Monetization hooks

This is a **shipping-focused MVP**, not feature soup.
